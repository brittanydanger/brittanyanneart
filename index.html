Meet Brittany

From the time I could hold a pencil, I knew art was my language. But it wasn't until I began listening — really listening — to the quiet voice within that my work became something more than technique. It became a channel.

Every portrait I create begins with intention and prayer. Whether I'm working from your vision or channeling one from spirit, my process is the same: I open my heart, connect with the energy of the subject, and let my hands follow. The result is a portrait that captures not just a likeness, but an essence — the part of you that the eye cannot see.

I believe art has the power to heal, to honor, and to make the invisible visible. It's my life's purpose to create that for you.

With love,

Commission a Portrait

A healing portrait is a visual representation of your emotional journey, created by a spiritually in-tune artist. Each piece includes symbolic elements and themes that represent growth, faith, or resilience.

Many are loss portraits — created in honor of loved ones who have passed, depicted in Heaven, with the Savior, or with a more traditional background. Some honor survivors of abuse, betrayal, or breakthroughs. Others exist to call in a brand new season.

You don't have to know exactly what you want. As an NDE survivor, trauma survivor, and professional artist for 13 years, it is my life's passion to transform pain and emotion into art. All you need is to be willing to share your story with me — and we'll build something glorious from there.
